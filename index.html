Digital Art


Kolam is a traditional Indian Sacred Geometry Art composed of curved loops and lines in Fibonacci series. These intricate patterns are believed to be the geometric manifestations of soothing sound vibrations. Being a Kolam artist from childhood, I blend the soothing symmetric patterns of Nature with mandalas in Digital platform. In my website ojasvishambhu.com , my learning related to this field is documented and free high-resolution images of my artwork is available.


My art is showcased in India's most popular portal for kolam art ikolam.com and other websites like Shutter Stock, Fine Art America , Devian Art
